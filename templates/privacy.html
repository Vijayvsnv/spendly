Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. When you use the Service, we collect the expense data you enter — including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions.

We do not collect payment information, government IDs, or any sensitive financial credentials.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the Service — displaying your expenses, generating summaries, and letting you filter and manage your records. We do not use your data for advertising or sell it to third parties.

We may use anonymised, aggregated statistics (e.g., average number of expense categories per user) to improve the product. This data cannot be traced back to you.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secure servers. We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect it from unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure.

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your data will be permanently removed within 30 days.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly uses a small number of third-party services to operate — such as hosting providers. These services are bound by their own privacy policies and are not permitted to use your data for any purpose beyond delivering their service to us.

We do not integrate with advertising networks, data brokers, or social media tracking pixels.

5. Cookies

We use session cookies to keep you logged in. These are essential to the Service and are not used for tracking. We do not use analytics cookies or third-party tracking cookies.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete the personal data we hold about you at any time. You can manage most of this directly from your account settings, or contact us to request changes.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please reach out at support@spendly.app.
